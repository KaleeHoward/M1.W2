Sourdough Brownies

These are always a hit with kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 8 Tbsp Butter
  • 12 Oz Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Half Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Half Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Half Cup Sourdough Discard**
  • 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the Butter, Vanilla Extract, Eggs and Yolks, Granulated and Brown Sugars, and the Sourdough Discard.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the Cocoa Powder, All Purpose Flour, and Salt.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then fold in the Chocolate Chips.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased 9x9 inch baking pan.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
  8. Enjoy your delicious sourdough brownies!
